True Purpose of the Study

This study examined how people perceive gradual changes when information is presented sequentially over time. We were specifically testing the "boiling frog" effect — the idea that people have difficulty noticing problems that develop gradually. We compared whether binary data (lake freeze: yes/no) versus continuous data (temperature numbers) helps people better detect gradual warming trends.

Why We Didn't Fully Explain This Beforehand

Knowing the true purpose might have influenced how you viewed the data, which could have affected the validity of our results.

What We Measured

Your perceptions of climate impact, warming, and change detection in the fictional town of Townsville.
